Overview

Lane Management is a system that controls and optimizes the use of road lanes to reduce congestion and improve safety. It makes sure traffic flows smoothly by guiding vehicles into the right lanes.

Key Features

  • Dynamic lane direction control during peak hours
  • Dedicated lanes for buses, carpools, and emergency vehicles
  • Real-time monitoring of each lane using sensors
  • Automatic detection of lane violations
  • Adaptive traffic signal timing based on lane usage

How It Works

The system uses cameras, sensors, and AI to watch the road. It counts vehicles in each lane, checks for violations, and can change lane directions or open special lanes when traffic gets heavy.

Benefits

  • Faster travel times
  • Less traffic congestion
  • Improved road safety
  • Efficient use of road space
