Overview

Lane Management helps organize the movement of vehicles on multi-lane roads. It improves traffic flow, reduces accidents, and allows emergency vehicles to move faster.

Key Features

  • Dynamic lane assignment based on traffic density
  • Priority lanes for public transport and emergency vehicles
  • Real-time lane switching alerts to drivers
  • Smart sensors to detect lane blockages

How It Works

Cameras and road sensors detect vehicle speed and density. If one lane is overcrowded, the system allows vehicles to shift to another lane and updates traffic lights to balance the flow.
